AR Commerce: The HTML Injection Breakthrough

Redefining the boundaries of 3D immersive shopping.

  • The Blueprint:  At the time, AR environments (PlayCanvas/8th Wall) were "walled gardens." To make a purchase, users were redirected to external websites, breaking the immersion and hurting conversion rates.
  • The Breakthrough:  I engineered a method to inject a responsive HTML/CSS layer directly into the AR coordinate system—a feat previously considered impossible in the browser. This allowed users to select products and checkout without ever leaving the AR experience, creating a seamless, "future-studio" shopping flow.
